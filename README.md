# Discount Calculator

A simple calculator where you can calculate the final price of a product with discounts! Especially useful when shopping :)

It also has some animations, even though they're not super cool! Like the animation when you change your input and money appears on the screen!

I also used some icons and fonts from `https://fonts.google.com/icons` to make it look cooler!

## Tips:
- Don't try to add the "$" or "%" symbols, they're not necessary! (It seems you can't even insert them, but anyway)
